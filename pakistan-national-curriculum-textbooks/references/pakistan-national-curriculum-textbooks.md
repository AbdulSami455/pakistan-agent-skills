# Pakistan National Curriculum and Textbooks Reference

## Source

- Ministry of Federal Education and Professional Training / National Curriculum documents: `https://mofept.gov.pk`
- Pakistan Institute of Education where curriculum support material may be hosted: `https://pie.gov.pk`
- Federal and provincial textbook boards for approved textbook lists and subject mappings

## Common Content Types

- subject-wise curriculum documents
- grade-wise learning outcomes
- content strands and competencies
- textbook approval lists
- board-specific textbook mappings

## Typical Dimensions

- class / grade
- subject
- compulsory vs elective
- curriculum edition / revision year
- board or jurisdiction

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve whether the source is a curriculum framework or a textbook approval list.
- Grade-level references should be exact because many subjects repeat across classes with different scope.
- Where a board publishes only textbooks and not full curriculum text, avoid overstating what the board document proves about content scope.

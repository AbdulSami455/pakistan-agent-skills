# PCAA Statistical Digest Reference

## Source

- Pakistan Civil Aviation Authority (PCAA)
- PCAA official website: `https://www.caapakistan.gov.pk`
- Statistical Digest and traffic statistics: `https://caapakistan.gov.pk/` (under the "Statistics" or "Publications" section)

## Structure Notes

- Airport-wise traffic summary (passenger, aircraft movement, cargo)
- Airline-wise traffic breakdown (domestic and foreign carriers)
- International versus domestic traffic split
- Cargo and mail tonnage tables
- Multi-year historical annex tables

## Major Airports Typically Covered

- Jinnah International Airport, Karachi
- Allama Iqbal International Airport, Lahore
- Islamabad International Airport
- Bacha Khan International Airport, Peshawar
- Quetta International Airport
- Other regional/domestic airports as traffic warrants

## Metric Definitions

- Passenger movement: count of passengers embarking/disembarking (often split into arrival/departure or domestic/international).
- Aircraft movement: count of landings and takeoffs.
- Cargo tonnage: weight of freight and mail handled, usually in metric tons.

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its fiscal year and specific airport/airline before quoting it.
- Treat any year with major disruption (e.g., pandemic-era travel restrictions) as a special case requiring explicit context rather than a like-for-like comparison.

# Pakistan Forests and Land Cover Reference

## Source

- Provincial forest departments
- Ministry / federal climate and forestry publications
- Official mangrove and watershed-forestry program references
- Government-adopted land-cover assessments where explicitly published for Pakistan

## Common Content Types

- forest-area statistics
- plantation and afforestation summaries
- mangrove management and area estimates
- watershed and rangeland descriptions
- land-cover classification summaries

## Typical Units

- hectares
- acres
- percent of land area

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve whether the figure is an estimate, mapped classification, planted area, or administrative target.
- Mangrove reporting is often concentrated in Sindh coastal systems; keep coastal context explicit.
- Where official programs publicize targets and achievements together, separate the two carefully.

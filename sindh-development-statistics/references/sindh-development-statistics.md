# Sindh Development Statistics Reference

## Source

- Sindh Bureau of Statistics, Government of Sindh.
- Published annually as "Sindh Development Statistics."
- Sindh Bureau of Statistics: `https://sbos.sindh.gov.pk` (or under `https://sindh.gov.pk` Statistics section) — (verify) confirm current live domain before citing, as provincial bureau subdomains occasionally change.

## Administrative Geography

- Sindh is organized into **6 divisions**: **Karachi**, **Hyderabad**, **Sukkur**, **Larkana**, **Mirpurkhas**, **Shaheed Benazirabad** (formerly Nawabshah division).
- Representative district examples outside Karachi: Hyderabad, Jamshoro, Thatta, Sujawal (Hyderabad division); Sukkur, Ghotki, Khairpur (Sukkur division); Larkana, Shikarpur, Kashmore, Jacobabad (Larkana division); Mirpurkhas, Umerkot, Tharparkar (Mirpurkhas division); Shaheed Benazirabad, Naushahro Feroze, Sanghar (Shaheed Benazirabad division).
- **Karachi division/district reorganization pitfall**: Karachi's internal district structure has been redrawn multiple times and is a well-known data-comparability trap when building multi-year series. Karachi was originally a single district, then split into five districts (Central, East, South, West, Malir) in 2000, later had a District Korangi split out from District East/Malir around 2011, and has seen further local-government-driven boundary and naming changes since (including periods where Karachi was administered as a unified "Karachi Metropolitan Corporation" area rather than as separate districts for some reporting purposes). Practical implication: a "Karachi East" or "Karachi Malir" figure from one year's report may not map cleanly onto the same-named district in another year's report, and pre-2000 "Karachi" figures are province/single-district figures that cannot be directly summed or compared against post-split per-district figures. Always confirm which Karachi sub-district structure a given edition uses before constructing a time series that spans a reorganization point.

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its administrative level (district/province) and reference year.
- Be explicit about whether a figure includes or excludes Karachi division when summarizing "Sindh" statistics, since Karachi's urban profile can skew province-wide averages.

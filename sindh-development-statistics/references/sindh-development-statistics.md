# Sindh Development Statistics Reference

## Source

- Sindh Bureau of Statistics, Government of Sindh.
- Published annually as "Sindh Development Statistics."
- Sindh Bureau of Statistics: `https://sindhbos.gov.pk` (or under `https://sindh.gov.pk` Statistics section)

## Structure Notes

- Area and population
- Agriculture and livestock
- Industry and manufacturing
- Education
- Health
- Labour force and employment
- Prices
- Transport and communication
- Energy
- Finance and banking
- Social welfare and other sectors

## Geography Levels

- Province-wide
- District-wide (Karachi's districts are often presented distinctly from other Sindh districts given the urban/rural contrast)

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its administrative level (district/province) and reference year.
- Be explicit about whether a figure includes or excludes Karachi division when summarizing "Sindh" statistics, since Karachi's urban profile can skew province-wide averages.

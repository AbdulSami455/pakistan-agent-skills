# I2I Startup Ecosystem Reference

## Source

- Invest2Innovate (I2I): `https://i2i.pk`
- Published periodically as "Pakistan Startup Ecosystem Report"; known editions have appeared roughly around 2014, 2016, 2019, 2021, and 2024 — always confirm the latest available edition before relying on it.

## Structure Notes

- Ecosystem overview and methodology
- Funding trend statistics (deal counts, disclosed value, year-over-year trend)
- Sector breakdown (fintech, e-commerce, logistics, healthtech, edtech, agritech, etc.)
- Stage breakdown (pre-seed, seed, Series A+)
- Ecosystem-player directory (VCs, angel networks, accelerators, incubators, donor programs)
- City-level concentration (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, others)
- Qualitative commentary on regulatory environment and talent pipeline

## Data Source Notes

- Funding statistics often draw on secondary aggregators (e.g., Crunchbase, Tracxn, Data Darbar) in addition to I2I's own primary research; check whether a specific edition discloses its data sourcing.

## Extraction Notes

- Always pair a funding or ecosystem figure with its specific report edition (year) and stated reference period.
- Sector and stage taxonomies may shift between editions; do not assume identical definitions when building a cross-edition trend.

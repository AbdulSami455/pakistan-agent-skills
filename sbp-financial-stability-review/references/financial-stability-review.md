# Financial Stability Review Reference

## Source

- State Bank of Pakistan, Financial Stability Review (annual): `https://www.sbp.org.pk` under Publications / Financial Stability Review.

## Typical Review Structure

- Overview/executive summary of systemic risk themes for the year.
- Banking sector chapter: credit risk, liquidity risk, market risk, and capital buffers at a systemic level.
- Non-bank financial institutions (NBFIs) chapter: leasing companies, investment banks, microfinance banks, and DFIs.
- Insurance sector commentary, often a shorter section.
- Systemic risk survey results, summarizing perceptions from financial-sector practitioners on top risks.
- Stress-testing section, presenting scenario-based resilience analysis for the banking sector.

## Indicator Guide

- systemic risk themes (credit, liquidity, market, concentration)
- stress-test scenario assumptions and resilience results
- NBFI sector-specific vulnerabilities
- insurance sector risk commentary
- systemic risk survey rankings of top perceived risks

## Extraction Notes

- The review is published annually and reflects conditions as of a stated reference date, which may lag the publication date by several months.
- Stress-test results describe hypothetical shock scenarios (e.g. a defined deterioration in asset quality or interest rates) and their effect on capital ratios; always preserve the scenario assumptions alongside the result.
- Systemic risk survey results are based on practitioner perception surveys and should be labeled as such, not presented as factual risk levels.
- Cross-reference specific CAR/NPL figures cited in the narrative against `sbp-financial-soundness-indicators` data for the same period when exact figures matter.

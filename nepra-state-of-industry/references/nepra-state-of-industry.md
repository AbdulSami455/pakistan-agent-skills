# NEPRA State of Industry Reference

## Source

- National Electric Power Regulatory Authority (NEPRA), Government of Pakistan
- NEPRA official website: `https://www.nepra.org.pk`
- Publications (Annual Reports and State of Industry Reports): `https://nepra.org.pk/publications/Annual%20Reports.php`
- State of Industry Report is published annually as a standalone PDF alongside NEPRA's Annual Report.

## Document Structure

- Generation chapter: installed capacity, dependable capacity, and actual energy generated, broken down by fuel type (thermal, hydel, nuclear, renewable) and by generation company/IPP.
- Transmission chapter: transmission system performance, losses, and NTDC-level infrastructure summary.
- Distribution chapter: DISCO-wise T&D losses, recovery/collection ratios, and supply performance.
- Financial performance chapter: sector revenue requirement, tariff differential subsidy, and circular debt commentary.
- Regulatory/licensing activity summary for the reporting year.

## Key Indicator Types

- installed vs. dependable capacity (MW) by fuel type
- actual energy generated (GWh) by fuel type and company
- DISCO-wise T&D losses (%) and recovery/collection ratio (%)
- supply reliability/outage statistics where reported
- circular debt and tariff differential subsidy figures

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to the specific fiscal year the SOI edition covers.
- Keep installed capacity, dependable capacity, and actual generation conceptually distinct — the report presents all three separately.
- DISCO-wise figures should be checked against the national aggregate for consistency before combining across companies.
- Cross-reference `igcep-capacity-plan` only for forward-looking planning context; do not blend planned and realized figures in the same statement without labeling each.

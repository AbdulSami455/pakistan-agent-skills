## Chapter Map

## Section Entry Points

- Use `8.2 Balance of Payment Performance` around survey pages `139-140` for current account, financial account, and reserves.
- Use `8.3 Merchandise Trade` around survey page `140` when the question is about customs-recorded goods trade rather than balance-of-payments framing.
- Use `8.3.1 Exports` around survey pages `141-143`.
- Use `8.3.2 Imports` around survey pages `143-146`.
- Use `8.3.3 Direction of Trade` around survey page `147` when the question is destination or partner specific.
- Use the measures for external-sector sustainability later in the chapter when the question is about government support actions rather than realized flows.

## Key Indicator Types

- goods exports
- services exports
- imports
- workers' remittances
- current account balance
- reserves and external buffers
- sustainability or support measures

## Extraction Notes

- Keep `surplus` and `deficit` language explicit.
- Preserve `US $` units exactly.
- Many figures are period-specific; do not annualize unless asked.
- Distinguish pre-crisis and post-crisis passages where the chapter does so.
- The chapter explicitly distinguishes PBS customs-based merchandise trade from SBP balance-of-payments presentation; do not merge them casually.
- When the answer needs a trade deficit figure, verify whether it is the merchandise trade deficit or current account balance.

# University Rankings Reference

## Source

- HEC University Rankings: published by the Higher Education Commission of Pakistan (`hec.gov.pk`), under the Quality Assurance / University Ranking section. Released in cycles, not annually.
- QS World University Rankings: published by Quacquarelli Symonds (`topuniversities.com`). Annual release (typically June for World, November for Asia).
- Times Higher Education World University Rankings: published by TES Global (`timeshighereducation.com`). Annual release (typically September).

## Ranking Systems — Pakistani University Coverage

### HEC Ranking
- Domestic ranking; covers all HEC-recognized public and private universities.
- Universities are grouped into categories: general, agriculture & veterinary, engineering & technology, medical, business education, and others.
- Ranking is within-category, not national overall.
- Updated in cycles — check HEC's Quality Assurance Agency (QAA) page for the latest edition.

### QS World University Rankings
- Global ranking; only a small number of Pakistani universities appear (typically NUST, LUMS, QAU, PU, UET Lahore, Aga Khan University, depending on the year).
- QS Asia ranking includes more Pakistani universities (often 10–20+).
- QS by Subject provides discipline-level positions for Pakistani institutions.

### Times Higher Education (THE)
- Global ranking; Pakistani representation is limited (typically NUST, LUMS, QAU, a few others).
- THE Asia ranking includes more Pakistani universities.
- THE Impact ranking (SDG-based) sometimes features additional Pakistani universities not in the main World ranking.

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a rank to its specific edition/year — e.g., "QS World 2025" or "HEC Ranking Cycle 2023".
- Pakistani universities may appear in QS Asia but not QS Global — always specify which list.
- HEC ranking positions are category-specific — a university ranked #1 in engineering is not necessarily #1 nationally.
- Subject/discipline rankings (QS by Subject, THE by Subject) are separate from overall university rankings.
- Do not average or merge positions across QS, THE, and HEC — they use different methodologies and scales.

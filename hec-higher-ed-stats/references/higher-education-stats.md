# Higher Education Stats Reference

## Source

- HEC Higher Education Data Repository (HEDR), `hedr.hec.gov.pk` and `hec.gov.pk`
- HEC Annual Report data tables/annexes

## Document/Portal Structure

- HEDR is queryable by university, by province, by sector (public/private), and by academic year; results render as data tables rather than narrative text.
- HEC Annual Report typically includes annex tables covering: number of chartered/recognized universities by province and sector, total enrollment by degree level, total graduates by degree level, faculty counts by qualification and rank, and a section listing HEC's university ranking/categorization results for the latest cycle.
- Ranking/categorization sections group universities into categories such as general, agriculture & veterinary sciences, engineering & technology, medical, and business education, each with its own ranked list.

## Key Indicator Types

- enrollment by university, discipline, and degree level (BS, MS/MPhil, PhD)
- graduate/degree-completion counts by degree level
- faculty headcount by qualification (PhD vs non-PhD) and academic rank
- count of chartered/recognized universities by province and sector
- university ranking/category placement by cycle

## Extraction Notes

- Always state the degree level when citing enrollment or graduate figures.
- Public-sector and private-sector data may be reported on different cycles; confirm before combining.
- Ranking categories are not directly comparable across categories (e.g. a "general" category rank is not comparable to an "engineering & technology" category rank).
- HEDR portal data can be more current than the printed Annual Report; note which source a figure came from.

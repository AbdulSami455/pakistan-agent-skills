# PBS National Accounts Reference

## Source

- Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS)
- Official website: `https://www.pbs.gov.pk`
- Quarterly National Accounts: `https://www.pbs.gov.pk/content/quarterly-national-accounts`
- Annual National Accounts: check PBS "National Accounts" or "Publications" section

## Typical Release Structure (QNA)

- GDP at current prices and constant prices
- Overall GDP growth rate (quarter and fiscal-year-to-date)
- Sectoral value added: agriculture, industry, services
- Expenditure-side components: private consumption, gross fixed capital formation, government consumption, exports, imports
- Provisional and revised estimates (revision schedule varies by release)

## Common Units and Labels

- Growth rates: percent (quarter-on-quarter, year-on-year, or fiscal-year-to-date)
- GDP levels: billion rupees (current or constant prices)
- Base year: noted in each release (Pakistan has rebased periodically; confirm the base year for the edition in use)

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its reference quarter/fiscal year, price basis (current/constant), and release vintage (provisional/revised).
- For cross-year comparisons spanning a rebasing event, do not chain constant-price levels across different base years without noting the break.

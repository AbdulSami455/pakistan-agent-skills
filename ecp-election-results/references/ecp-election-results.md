# ECP Election Results Reference

## Source

- Election Commission of Pakistan official website: `https://www.ecp.gov.pk`
- Use the ECP result pages, notifications, and constituency references available on the official site.

## Result Types

- Provisional result: early announced tally, may still change after consolidation or review.
- Consolidated result: official tally after returning officer consolidation.
- Final official result: published result or gazette-style notice that should be treated as authoritative.

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve the seat number, constituency name, election year, and province exactly as published.
- If the result is for a by-election, include the by-election date and note that it is not a general election result.
- If a result is reported through a scanned notice or form, transcribe figures carefully and note the source type.

# IGCEP Capacity Plan Reference

## Source

- Prepared by NTDC/NPCC (National Transmission and Despatch Company / National Power Control Centre); reviewed and approved by NEPRA (`https://nepra.org.pk`).
- Published periodically as new editions; check NEPRA's website for the latest approved IGCEP and any interim updates.

## Known Editions (Indicative)

- IGCEP 2021-30
- IGCEP 2022-31
- IGCEP 2024-34
- Later editions may exist; always confirm the most recent approved version before relying on its forecasts.

## Structure Notes

- Demand forecast and load growth assumptions
- Existing and committed generation inventory
- Candidate project list with technology, capacity, and indicative commissioning year
- Least-cost expansion plan and alternative scenarios
- Fuel mix and cost/tariff assumption inputs
- Transmission adequacy implications
- Sensitivity analysis (demand, hydrology, fuel price)

## Category Definitions

- Existing: already commissioned and operating capacity.
- Committed: contracted/financially closed projects expected to come online regardless of this plan's recommendation.
- Candidate: projects considered for selection by the least-cost optimization but not yet committed.

## Extraction Notes

- Always state the edition and horizon together, e.g., "IGCEP 2024-34."
- NEPRA may approve an edition with modifications; check whether a figure is from NTDC's submission or NEPRA's approved version if both are referenced.

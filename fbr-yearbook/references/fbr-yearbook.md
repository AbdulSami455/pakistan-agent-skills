# FBR Year Book Reference

## Source

- Federal Board of Revenue, Year Book (annual): `https://www.fbr.gov.pk` under Publications / Year Book.

## Typical Year Book Structure

- Overview/highlights: summary of total collection and year-on-year growth.
- Direct taxes chapter: income tax collection, including withholding, voluntary payments, and collection on demand.
- Indirect taxes chapters: sales tax (domestic and imports), federal excise duty, and customs duty, each with sub-tables.
- Sector-wise collection tables: contribution by major industries/sectors to total tax collection.
- Withholding tax tables: collection by withholding category (salary, contracts, imports, exports, dividends, profit on debt, etc.).
- Multi-year time-series annex: historical collection trends by tax head.

## Indicator Guide

- total FBR tax collection (gross and net of refunds)
- income tax collection (withholding vs. voluntary vs. collection on demand)
- sales tax collection (domestic vs. imports)
- federal excise duty collection
- customs duty collection
- sector-wise tax contribution
- withholding tax by category

## Extraction Notes

- "Gross collection" and "net collection" (after refunds) are both commonly reported; always specify which is being cited.
- Provisional figures reported early in the fiscal year may be revised in later editions; prefer the most recent Year Book edition covering the same year unless the task needs the originally reported figure.
- Sector classifications can shift slightly between editions; note any reclassification when comparing multi-year trends.
- Withholding tax tables are often the most granular source for category-specific questions (e.g. withholding on property transactions, contracts, or imports).

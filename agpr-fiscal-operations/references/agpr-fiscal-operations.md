# AGPR Fiscal Operations Reference

## Official Sources

- Finance Division, Government of Pakistan: `https://www.finance.gov.pk/`
- Accountant General Pakistan Revenues (AGPR): use the official Government of Pakistan finance/accounts portals for accounts and fiscal-operation releases.

## Common Source Families

- fiscal operations statements
- monthly or periodic accounts releases
- treasury and cash-balance updates
- federal receipts and expenditure summaries
- financing and borrowing tables

## Interpretation Notes

- Some releases are cash-flow oriented and some are accounting oriented; keep the basis explicit.
- Federal-only and consolidated figures are not interchangeable.
- Current expenditure, development expenditure, and financing should be treated as separate concepts.
- If the source reports cumulative year-to-date values, do not present them as a single-month value.

## Extraction Notes

- Always capture the reporting period, fiscal year, and table title.
- Preserve whether the figure is gross, net, cumulative, or period-specific.
- If the source includes revised figures, keep the revision vintage.
- When a figure is derived from multiple components, note the component list rather than collapsing it into a single line.

## Common Data Families

- receipts
- current expenditure
- development expenditure
- deficit / surplus
- domestic financing
- external financing
- cash balance

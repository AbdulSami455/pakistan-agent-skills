# Pakistan Railways Yearbook Reference

## Source

- Pakistan Railways, Ministry of Railways, Government of Pakistan; check the Ministry/Pakistan Railways official website for the publications section hosting the Yearbook.

## Structure Notes

- Passenger traffic: passengers carried, passenger-kilometers, by class and route
- Freight traffic: tonnage carried, ton-kilometers, by commodity group
- Locomotive and rolling stock inventory and utilization
- Financial performance: revenue, expenses, subsidy, profit/loss
- Infrastructure: track length, stations, electrification status
- Safety statistics in some editions

## Commodity Groups (Typical Freight Categories)

- Cement
- Coal
- POL (petroleum, oil, lubricants)
- Containerized/general goods
- Other bulk commodities

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its fiscal year.
- Distinguish raw counts (passengers, tons) from distance-weighted measures (passenger-km, ton-km).
- Note whether financial performance figures are stated gross or net of government subsidy.

# ADB Pakistan Indicators Reference

## Source

- Asian Development Bank (ADB)
- Key Indicators for Asia and the Pacific: `https://www.adb.org/publications/series/key-indicators-for-asia-and-the-pacific`
- Basic Statistics: `https://www.adb.org/publications/series/basic-statistics`
- ADB Data Library: `https://data.adb.org`
- ADB Pakistan country page: `https://www.adb.org/countries/pakistan/main`
- Pakistan economic updates and sector reports: published under ADB Pakistan country publications

## Typical Indicator Categories

- Macro: GDP growth, inflation, fiscal balance, current account
- Poverty and inequality: poverty rate, Gini coefficient
- Trade and finance: exports, imports, FDI, remittances
- Social: life expectancy, school enrollment, literacy
- Infrastructure: access to electricity, road density, water supply coverage
- Sector-specific: energy, transport, agriculture, health, education indicators in ADB sector reports

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its reference year, indicator definition, and publication source.
- Note whether the value is an ADB estimate or a reported national value.
- Confirm the poverty line definition when citing poverty rates.
- ADB editions may revise historical series; confirm the publication vintage in use.

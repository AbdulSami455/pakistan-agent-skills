# Gilgit-Baltistan Development Statistics Reference

## Source

- Planning & Development Department, Government of Gilgit-Baltistan
- Published annually as "Gilgit-Baltistan Development Statistics" (sometimes also issued in condensed form as "GB in Figures")
- Official website: `https://gb.gov.pk` (Planning & Development section)
- Publications section: check the Department's "Publications" or "Statistical Reports" page for the latest edition

## Structure Notes

- Area and population
- Agriculture and livestock
- Industry and manufacturing
- Education
- Health
- Labour force and employment
- Prices
- Transport and communication
- Energy
- Finance and banking
- Social welfare and other sectors

## Geography Levels

- Territory-wide
- District-wide
(Confirm administrative boundary version for the edition in use.)

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its administrative level (district/territory) and reference year.
- Condensed "in Figures" booklets are quick-reference summaries; for full time series and district breakdowns, the main Development Statistics volume is the authoritative source.
- GB is a distinct administrative territory, not a province; preserve this label when citing figures.

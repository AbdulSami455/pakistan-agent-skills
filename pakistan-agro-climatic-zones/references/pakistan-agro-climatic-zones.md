# Pakistan Agro-Climatic Zones Reference

## Source

- Pakistan Agricultural Research Council (PARC): `https://www.parc.gov.pk`
- Provincial agriculture extension and agronomy references
- Official agro-ecological and crop-zoning publications used by Pakistan agricultural agencies

## Common Content Types

- agro-climatic zone descriptions
- crop suitability matrices
- sowing and harvest calendars
- irrigation and rainfall regime notes
- crop-sequence and cropping-pattern guidance

## Key Dimensions

- Kharif vs Rabi
- irrigated vs barani / rainfed
- lowland plains vs high-altitude valleys
- temperature and rainfall suitability
- soil and salinity constraints

## Extraction Notes

- Keep agronomic recommendations distinct from realized output data.
- Preserve local terminology such as barani where the source uses it.
- Seasonal windows may shift by region; attach them to the right geography.

# Punjab Development Statistics Reference

## Source

- Punjab Bureau of Statistics, Government of Punjab
- Published annually as "Punjab Development Statistics" (also issued as "Punjab in Figures")
- Official website: `https://bos.gop.pk` (Bureau of Statistics, Government of Punjab)
- Publications section: `https://bos.gop.pk/publications`

## Structure Notes

- Area and population
- Agriculture and livestock
- Industry and manufacturing
- Education
- Health
- Labour force and employment
- Prices
- Transport and communication
- Energy
- Finance and banking
- Social welfare and other sectors

## Geography Levels

- Province-wide
- Division-wide
- District-wide
(Confirm administrative boundary version for the edition in use, as district/division reorganizations occur periodically.)

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its administrative level (district/division/province) and reference year.
- "Punjab in Figures" is a quick-reference summary; for full time series and district breakdowns, the main Development Statistics volume is the authoritative source.

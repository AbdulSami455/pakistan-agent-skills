# SBP Annual Report Reference

## Source

- State Bank of Pakistan, Annual Report ("State of the Economy" plus statistical annexes): `https://www.sbp.org.pk` under Publications / Annual Report.

## Typical Report Structure

- Governor's foreword or overview.
- State of the Economy chapters covering: real sector, fiscal sector, money and banking, capital markets, and external sector, told from SBP's perspective.
- Special/boxed sections on topical issues (e.g. a specific policy reform or shock during the year).
- Statistical annex with detailed tables on monetary aggregates, banking sector balance sheets, government borrowing from the banking system, and balance of payments.

## Indicator Guide

- broad money (M2) growth and its components
- credit to private sector and government sector borrowing from banks
- banking sector deposit and advances growth
- SBP's own account of inflation drivers and monetary stance during the year
- external sector summary (current account, reserves) as told by SBP
- comparison points against budgeted/targeted figures where SBP comments on fiscal performance

## Extraction Notes

- The report year refers to the fiscal year covered (July-June), which may differ from the calendar year of publication.
- SBP's narrative often references its own MPC decisions during the year; cross-check exact rate levels against `sbp-monetary-policy-statement` if precision matters.
- Annex tables are the more reliable source for precise figures; narrative text may round or qualify numbers.
- When SBP discusses fiscal developments, it is commentary from the central bank's perspective, not the Ministry of Finance's own fiscal reporting in the PES.

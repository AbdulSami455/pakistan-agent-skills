# World Bank Pakistan Indicators Reference

## Source

- World Bank
- World Development Indicators (WDI): `https://datacatalog.worldbank.org/search/dataset/0037712`
- World Bank Data Portal (Pakistan): `https://data.worldbank.org/country/pakistan`
- Pakistan Development Update and other country reports: `https://www.worldbank.org/en/country/pakistan`

## Typical Indicator Categories

- Macro: GDP, GNI, growth rate, inflation (CPI), population
- Poverty and inequality: poverty headcount ratio, Gini index, shared prosperity
- Trade and finance: exports, imports, FDI, remittances, current account
- Social: life expectancy, school enrollment, literacy
- Infrastructure: access to electricity, improved water/sanitation
- Environment: CO2 emissions, forest area, renewable energy
- Governance: Worldwide Governance Indicators (WGI)

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its indicator code (if WDI), reference year, and definition.
- Note whether the value is a World Bank estimate or a reported national value.
- Confirm the poverty line definition when citing poverty headcount ratios.
- WDI editions may revise historical series; confirm the database vintage in use.

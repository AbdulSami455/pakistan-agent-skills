# PARC Reference

## Source

- Pakistan Agricultural Research Council (PARC), apex national agricultural research organization
- PARC official website: `http://www.parc.gov.pk`

## Organizational Structure

- Technical divisions: Plant Sciences, Animal Sciences, Social Sciences, Natural Resources, Agricultural Engineering.
- Services divisions: Finance, Coordination & Monitoring.
- Board of Governors: includes provincial Secretaries of Agriculture/Livestock/Forestry Departments, Vice Chancellors of agricultural universities, and farmer representation — reflecting PARC's coordinating role across the national agricultural research system rather than sole federal implementation.

## Core Functions

- Apex-level research coordination across federal and provincial agricultural research institutes and universities.
- Capacity building: training and workshops for farmers, researchers, and extension workers.
- Extension/technology transfer: facilitating the transfer of research findings and technologies to the farming community, distinct from PARC's own research-generation activity.

## Extraction Notes

- Keep PARC's research-coordination and extension role distinct from crop-production statistics (`mnfsr-agri-statistics`) and census-round farm-structure data (`pbs-agriculture-census`).
- Represent PARC as a coordinating apex body working with provincial institutes/universities, not as the sole implementer of Pakistani agricultural research.
- Distinguish extension/technology-transfer activity from core research generation when describing PARC's work.

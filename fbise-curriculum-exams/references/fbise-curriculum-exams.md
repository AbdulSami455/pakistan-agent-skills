# FBISE Reference

## Source

- Federal Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (FBISE), Government of Pakistan
- FBISE official website: `https://www.fbise.edu.pk`
- Syllabus and Model Question Papers (published per subject/level under the "Syllabus" navigation, e.g. SSC-I and HSSC-II pages)
- Past/old question papers section on the official website

## Document Structure

- Scheme of Studies: per-grade, per-stream subject list with marks/credit distribution.
- Syllabus documents: subject-wise content outline, often tied to Student Learning Outcomes (SLOs) under the National Curriculum of Pakistan.
- Model Question Papers: SLO-aligned sample papers per subject, showing section structure (MCQs, short questions, long questions) and marks weighting.
- Old/past question papers: actual prior-year exam papers, organized by level, subject, and year.
- Date sheets and notifications: exam schedule, registration, and result-related announcements.

## Key Distinctions

- SSC (Matric): Part-I (grade 9) and Part-II (grade 10).
- HSSC (Intermediate): Part-I (grade 11) and Part-II (grade 12).
- Streams: Science (Pre-Medical, Pre-Engineering/Computer Science), Humanities/Arts, Commerce — each with a distinct subject list.
- FBISE governs Islamabad and federally administered institutions only; provincial boards (e.g., Lahore Board, Karachi Board, Peshawar Board, Quetta Board, AJK Board) are separate, independently administered examining bodies not covered by this skill.

## Extraction Notes

- Always confirm level (SSC/HSSC), part (I/II), and stream before citing a subject list or marks distribution.
- Note the exact examination year when citing past papers or date sheets, since format and content can change across years, especially after curriculum/SLO reforms.
- Do not assume FBISE's syllabus or papers apply to a student under a different (provincial) board.

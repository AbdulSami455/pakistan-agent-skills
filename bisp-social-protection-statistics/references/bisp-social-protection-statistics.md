# BISP Social Protection Statistics Reference

## Source

- Benazir Income Support Programme (BISP)
- Official website: `https://bisp.gov.pk`
- Annual reports and statistical publications under "Publications" or "Reports" sections
- NSER (National Socio-Economic Registry) statistics may be published separately on the BISP website

## Typical Report Sections

- Total beneficiary households enrolled and active
- Payment disbursement totals (amount and transaction count) by period
- Beneficiary breakdown by province, district, gender of recipient
- NSER registration and survey coverage statistics
- Complementary programme coverage (Benazir Taleemi Wazaif, Benazir Nashonuma, etc.)

## Programme Name Evolution

- BISP (Benazir Income Support Programme): original programme name
- Ehsaas/Benazir Kafaalat: expanded branding under the Ehsaas umbrella
- Preserve the name used in the source document when citing figures

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its programme component, period, and geographic level.
- Distinguish household counts from individual counts.
- Distinguish enrolled/registered stock from active payment recipients for a given period.

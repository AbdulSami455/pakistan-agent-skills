# Pakistan School Infrastructure and WASH Reference

## Source

- AEPAM/NEMIS / Pakistan Institute of Education annual school census and facility tables
- Provincial EMIS and school-education department facility reports
- UNICEF-supported school WASH references where tied to official Pakistan education datasets

## Common Facility Indicators

- drinking water
- toilets / sanitation
- electricity
- boundary wall
- classrooms
- laboratories
- ICT / computer lab
- accessibility facilities where reported

## Common Dimensions

- province / district
- public vs private
- rural vs urban
- school level
- girls / boys / co-education where available

## Extraction Notes

- Always preserve whether the metric is a count or percentage.
- Infrastructure indicators may be inconsistent across editions; confirm exact wording before comparing years.
- Where "availability" and "functionality" are separate, do not merge them.

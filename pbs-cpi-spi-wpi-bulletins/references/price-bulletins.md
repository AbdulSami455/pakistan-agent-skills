# Price Bulletins Reference

## Source

- Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS): monthly CPI press release, weekly SPI bulletin, WPI bulletin
- Published at `pbs.gov.pk`

## Document Structure

- CPI press release: headline national CPI inflation (MoM and YoY), urban/rural CPI, group-wise indices (food and non-alcoholic beverages, housing/water/electricity/fuel, transport, clothing/footwear, etc.), and core inflation (often defined excluding food and energy).
- SPI bulletin: weekly index built from a fixed basket of essential/sensitive items, reported by income-group quintile and often by city/market, with week-on-week and year-on-year change.
- WPI bulletin: monthly wholesale-level index by commodity group, distinct from the CPI retail basket and classification.
- Each bulletin includes a summary table (headline figures) followed by detailed item/commodity/group tables.

## Key Indicator Types

- headline CPI inflation (MoM, YoY) and core inflation
- CPI group/sub-group indices
- SPI weekly item-level price change by income group and/or city
- WPI commodity-group indices

## Extraction Notes

- Always state which index (CPI, SPI, WPI) and which change basis (MoM, YoY, WoW) a figure represents.
- SPI's income-group and city-level detail should not be presented as a national CPI-equivalent figure.
- Base years differ by index and are periodically rebased; note the base year when quoting an index level.
- WPI and CPI use different commodity/group classifications; do not map one directly onto the other.

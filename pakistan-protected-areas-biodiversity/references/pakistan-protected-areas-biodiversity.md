# Pakistan Protected Areas and Biodiversity Reference

## Source

- Provincial wildlife and national parks departments
- Ministry / environmental authorities responsible for biodiversity strategy material
- Ramsar Sites Information Service for Pakistan wetland listings: `https://rsis.ramsar.org`
- IUCN Pakistan or officially cited biodiversity-planning documents where relevant support context exists

## Common Designation Types

- national park
- wildlife sanctuary
- game reserve
- Ramsar site / wetland of international importance

## Typical Attributes

- site name
- province or territory
- designation type
- managing authority
- habitat/ecosystem note
- notable biodiversity or species context

## Extraction Notes

- Keep legal designation and ecological description separate.
- Where provinces maintain their own lists, preserve province-specific naming conventions.
- If a site has multiple statuses, list each one explicitly rather than choosing one and dropping the rest.

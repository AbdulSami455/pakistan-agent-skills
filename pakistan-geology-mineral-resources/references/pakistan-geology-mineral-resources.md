# Pakistan Geology and Mineral Resources Reference

## Source

- Geological Survey of Pakistan: `https://www.gsp.gov.pk`
- Federal and provincial mines and minerals departments where official resource summaries or deposit lists are published
- Pakistan Mineral Development Corporation background material where official project or resource context is needed: `https://www.pmdc.gov.pk`

## Typical Content Types

- geological maps and explanatory notes
- mineral occurrence summaries by province or district
- basin and belt descriptions
- formation and stratigraphic references
- project-background notes for major resource areas

## Resource Categories Commonly Encountered

- metallic minerals such as copper, gold, iron ore, chromite
- industrial minerals such as gypsum, limestone, rock salt
- energy minerals such as coal
- dimension stone such as marble and granite
- gemstones and precious stones

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve whether the source is mapping geology, listing occurrences, or describing active projects.
- Keep source language conservative around reserve/resource certainty.
- Use district names carefully when old geological documents predate newer administrative splits.

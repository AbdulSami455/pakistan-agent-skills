# Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Development Statistics Reference

## Source

- Bureau of Statistics, Planning & Development Department, Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa
- Published annually as "Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Development Statistics" (sometimes also issued in condensed form as "KP in Figures")
- Official website: `https://bos.kp.gov.pk` (Bureau of Statistics, Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa)
- Publications section: check the Bureau's "Publications" or "Statistical Reports" page for the latest edition

## Structure Notes

- Area and population
- Agriculture and livestock
- Industry and manufacturing
- Education
- Health
- Labour force and employment
- Prices
- Transport and communication
- Energy
- Finance and banking
- Social welfare and other sectors

## Geography Levels

- Province-wide
- Division-wide
- District-wide
(Confirm administrative boundary version for the edition in use; KP has seen significant district reorganizations, especially after the 2018 merger of former FATA districts.)

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its administrative level (district/division/province) and reference year.
- Condensed "in Figures" booklets are quick-reference summaries; for full time series and district breakdowns, the main Development Statistics volume is the authoritative source.
- When comparing pre- and post-merger district data, map old administrative units explicitly rather than assuming continuity.

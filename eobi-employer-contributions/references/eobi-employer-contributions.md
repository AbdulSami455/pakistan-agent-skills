# EOBI Employer Contributions Reference

## Source

- Employees' Old-Age Benefits Institution: `https://www.eobi.gov.pk`
- EOBI employer registration and contribution guidance
- Employees' Old-Age Benefits Act and associated rules where referenced by EOBI

## Common Compliance Surfaces

- employer registration
- establishment coverage
- insured-person registration
- monthly contribution payment
- contribution record reconciliation

## Key Distinctions

- EOBI old-age benefits vs provincial social security
- employer contribution vs employee deduction
- payroll compliance vs employee benefit eligibility

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve whether the question is about coverage, contribution mechanics, or benefit-linked records.
- Where exact rates or ceilings are cited, tie them to the current EOBI instructions or legal framework rather than assuming historical values remain current.

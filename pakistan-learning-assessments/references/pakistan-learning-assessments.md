# Pakistan Learning Assessments Reference

## Source

- ASER Pakistan: `https://aserpakistan.org`
- Provincial student assessment systems where official reports are published
- Pakistan Institute of Education / ministry-supported learning assessment publications where available

## Common Indicator Types

- reading competency
- sentence or story reading at benchmark level
- arithmetic / numeracy competency
- subject-wise achievement scores or bands
- public vs private and rural vs urban comparisons

## Common Dimensions

- grade
- province or district
- gender
- school type
- assessment year

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve the benchmark wording because seemingly similar literacy indicators may measure different thresholds.
- Treat assessment results as performance indicators, not enrollment or attendance measures.
- If the report is district-based or sample-based, keep the scope explicit.

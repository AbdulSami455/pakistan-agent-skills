# UNICEF Pakistan Data Reference

## Source

- UNICEF
- UNICEF Data Portal (Pakistan): `https://data.unicef.org/country/pak`
- UNICEF Pakistan country office: `https://www.unicef.org/pakistan`
- Multiple Indicator Cluster Survey (MICS) Pakistan reports: published on the MICS website and UNICEF Pakistan publications page

## Typical Indicator Categories

- Child mortality (infant, under-five, neonatal mortality rates)
- Nutrition (stunting, wasting, overweight prevalence among children under five)
- Immunization coverage (BCG, DPT, measles, polio, etc.)
- WASH (access to improved drinking water, sanitation, handwashing facilities)
- Education (net attendance rate, out-of-school children, early childhood education)
- Child protection (birth registration, child labour, early marriage)

## MICS Survey Notes

- MICS is conducted periodically in Pakistan (survey rounds are not annual)
- Provides provincial and sometimes district-level breakdowns
- Confirm the survey round year before citing subnational figures

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its survey round or reference year and indicator definition.
- Note whether the value is a UNICEF estimate or a directly measured survey result.
- Preserve age bands and population scopes exactly as defined in the survey instrument.

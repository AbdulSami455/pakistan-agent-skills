# NADRA Reference

## Source

- National Database and Registration Authority (NADRA), Government of Pakistan
- NADRA official website: `https://www.nadra.gov.pk`
- Pak-Identity (online ID issuance service): `https://id.nadra.gov.pk/e-id/`
- Passport services are handled separately by the Directorate General of Immigration & Passports (DGIP), an attached department of the Ministry of Interior — not NADRA and not the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. See `https://dgip.gov.pk` for that separate process.

## Document Types

- CNIC (Computerized National Identity Card): mandatory for citizens 18+, issued under the NADRA Ordinance, 2000.
- Smart Card CNIC: chip-based variant of the CNIC with enhanced security features; same underlying legal document.
- NICOP (National Identity Card for Overseas Pakistanis): for Pakistani citizens residing abroad.
- POC (Pakistan Origin Card): for foreign nationals of Pakistani origin who do not hold Pakistani citizenship.
- CRC (Child Registration Certificate): registration document for minors, distinct from a CNIC.
- FRC (Family Registration Certificate): family-unit registration record.

## Application Channels

- In-person at NADRA registration centers within Pakistan.
- Overseas Pakistanis: designated overseas centers or Pakistani consulates/embassies, or NADRA's online Pak-Identity service where applicable.
- Processing tiers (normal, urgent, executive) typically differ in fee and turnaround time.

## Extraction Notes

- Always match the document type to the applicant's actual citizenship/residency situation before describing a process.
- Treat specific fees and processing times as subject to periodic revision; direct users to confirm current figures via NADRA's official channels.
- Do not describe passport application requirements as part of NADRA's service list — that is a separate agency's process.

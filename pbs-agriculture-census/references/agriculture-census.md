# Agriculture Census Reference

## Source

- Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS), Agricultural Census Organization
- "Pakistan Agricultural Census - Main Findings Report" and "Pakistan Livestock Census - Main Findings Report"
- Conducted roughly once a decade

## Document Structure

- Agricultural Census Main Findings Report: chapters/tables on number and area of farms by size class, land tenure type, irrigation source, cropping pattern at the holding level, farm machinery ownership, and agricultural credit/input use, generally broken down by province and sometimes district.
- Livestock Census Main Findings Report: tables on livestock counts by species (cattle, buffalo, sheep, goats, poultry, etc.), herd size distribution, and ownership patterns by province and district.
- Both reports present full enumeration counts (census), not sample-survey estimates.
- Reports are dated to a specific census round (e.g. the most recent agricultural census round); there is no annual update between rounds.

## Key Indicator Types

- number and area of farms by size class
- land tenure type (owner, owner-cum-tenant, tenant)
- irrigation source at the farm level
- livestock headcounts by species
- farm machinery ownership and input/credit access

## Extraction Notes

- Always state the census round/year; this is point-in-time structural data, not an annual series.
- Farm size class boundaries and tenure category definitions are specific to the census instrument; preserve them exactly rather than paraphrasing.
- Livestock figures are headcounts, not production volumes; do not conflate with milk/meat output statistics.
- Province and district totals should reconcile to the national total; investigate discrepancies before combining tables.

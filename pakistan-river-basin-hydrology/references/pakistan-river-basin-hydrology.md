# Pakistan River Basin Hydrology Reference

## Source

- Indus River System Authority (IRSA) daily operational data: `http://pakirsa.gov.pk`
- Pakistan Meteorological Department (PMD) Flood Forecasting Division bulletins: `https://www.pmd.gov.pk`
- WAPDA water and hydropower operational publications where available: `https://www.wapda.gov.pk`

## River System Scope

- Indus main stem
- Jhelum
- Chenab
- Ravi
- Sutlej
- Kabul

## Typical Operational Control Points

- reservoir outlets and linked inflow/outflow stations
- barrages such as Chashma, Taunsa, Guddu, Sukkur, and Kotri where reported
- canal heads where major withdrawals are tabulated

## Key Variable Types

- discharge / flow in cusecs
- inflow and outflow at control structures
- releases and withdrawals
- forecast river stages or flood-category warnings where PMD FFD provides them

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve the exact site name because hydrology bulletins often mix rivers, barrages, and canals in one table.
- Anchor every figure to a specific date and reporting surface.
- Keep basin geography explicit when comparing tributaries, especially in flood-season reporting.

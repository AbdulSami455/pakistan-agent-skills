# OGRA Petroleum Industry Reports Reference

## Source

- Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority (OGRA)
- Official website: `https://www.ogra.org.pk`
- Primary publication: annual "State of the Regulated Petroleum Industry" report
- Publications section: `https://www.ogra.org.pk/ogra-reports` (Annual Reports and State of Industry reports)

## Typical Report Sections

- Indigenous crude oil and natural gas production
- Petroleum product consumption by type (motor spirit, HSD, furnace oil, kerosene, etc.)
- Natural gas allocation and consumption by sector (power, fertilizer, domestic, CNG, industry)
- LPG and LNG supply/import statistics
- Pipeline and storage infrastructure
- Refinery throughput and capacity utilization
- Regulated consumer prices for petroleum products and natural gas

## Common Units

- Crude oil: barrels or metric tons
- Natural gas: MMCFD (million cubic feet per day) or BCM
- Petroleum products: metric tons, liters, or barrels
- LPG: metric tons or cylinders
- LNG: MMCFD or metric tons

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its product category, sector, and reference year/period.
- Distinguish indigenous production from imports and from total supply.
- Price tables may show multiple price types (regulated consumer price, import parity, producer price); preserve the price type label.

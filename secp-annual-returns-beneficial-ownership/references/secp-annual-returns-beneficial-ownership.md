# SECP Annual Returns and Beneficial Ownership Reference

## Source

- Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan: `https://www.secp.gov.pk`
- SECP eServices / Business Registration Portal: `https://eservices.secp.gov.pk`
- Companies Act, 2017 and associated SECP rules/regulations

## Common Compliance Surfaces

- annual return
- financial statement filing
- change in directors / officers
- change in shareholders / share capital
- registered office changes
- beneficial ownership declarations

## Key Concepts

- registered company status is not the same as current filing compliance
- legal shareholder and beneficial owner are not always the same person/entity
- event-driven changes trigger their own filings separate from annual cycle filings

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve whether the filing obligation is periodic or change-triggered.
- Distinguish public registry lookup from login-based compliance filing.
- Where exact form codes or deadlines matter, attach them to the specific company type and current SECP rules rather than generalizing.

## Section Entry Points

- Agriculture opens with crop performance and commodity tables around survey pages `21-25`.
- Use `2.1-a (ii) Other Crops` around survey page `24` for non-core crop tables.
- Use `2.1-b Livestock and Poultry` around survey pages `25-29` for livestock and poultry output.
- Manufacturing and Mining opens around survey page `43`.
- Use the group-wise manufacturing table near survey page `45` when the question is about weights, growth rates, or contribution points.
- Stay close to the nearby explanatory paragraphs after each table; they explain what is driving the measured increase or decline.

## Typical Data Shapes

- crop area and production tables
- provisional agricultural estimates
- manufacturing group tables with weights, growth rates, and contribution points
- narrative explanation of sector drivers
- commodity-specific import or availability discussion inside agriculture subsections
- industrial group commentary following tables

## Extraction Notes

- Always carry forward table units.
- Keep `(P)` or other estimate markers when they appear.
- For manufacturing tables, do not merge `weight`, `% change`, and `point contribution`.
- Use the surrounding paragraph to interpret the table only after extracting the raw figures accurately.
- Agriculture tables may switch between `000 hectares`, `000 acres`, and `000 tonnes`.
- Manufacturing sections often discuss `July-March FY 2026`; keep that period visible in the answer.

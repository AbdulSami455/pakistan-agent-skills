## Extraction Notes

- Many chapter openers are infographic-heavy; confirm the figure in chapter text when precision matters.
- Preserve institution names exactly when the survey attributes delivery to a specific entity.
- Keep infrastructure indicators, digital indicators, and climate narrative distinct unless the chapter explicitly links them.
- IT and telecom sections often contain policy, operational, and regulatory material in the same run of pages; identify which layer the question is asking about before answering.
- Energy sections can mix consumption, generation, project pipeline, and policy transition; do not quote one as if it answers the others.

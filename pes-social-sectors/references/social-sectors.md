## Key Indicator Types

- enrolment, institutions, and teacher counts
- registered doctors, dentists, nurses, and other health personnel
- population size and growth
- labour-force and employment discussion
- poverty, inequality, and welfare indicators

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve age bands, provincial scope, and source-specific labels.
- Health manpower tables may be reported on a calendar-year basis.
- Poverty and inequality discussion may combine national and provincial measures; keep them separate.
- Education sections may mix actual and estimated enrolment years; keep the school year or estimate basis explicit.
- Social protection sections often pair welfare deterioration with progress in selected social indicators; preserve that contrast instead of flattening it.

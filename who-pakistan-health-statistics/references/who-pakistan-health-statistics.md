# WHO Pakistan Health Statistics Reference

## Source

- World Health Organization (WHO)
- WHO Pakistan country profile: `https://www.who.int/countries/pak`
- World Health Statistics: `https://www.who.int/data/gho/publications/world-health-statistics`
- WHO Global Health Observatory (GHO) data: `https://www.who.int/data/gho/data/countries/country-details/GHO/pakistan`
- WHO Pakistan country office: `https://www.who.int/pakistan`

## Typical Indicator Categories

- Life expectancy and healthy life expectancy
- Maternal, neonatal, and under-five mortality
- Immunization coverage (DTP3, measles, polio, etc.)
- Disease burden (TB, malaria, HIV/AIDS incidence/prevalence)
- Health expenditure (percent of GDP, per capita, government share)
- Health workforce density (physicians, nurses, midwives per 10,000 population)
- Hospital beds per 10,000 population
- Cause-of-death distribution

## Extraction Notes

- Always anchor a figure to its reference year and indicator definition.
- Note whether the value is a WHO estimate, a reported national value, or a modeled projection.
- WHO may revise historical series; confirm the edition or database vintage in use.

# Pakistan Gazette Notifications Reference

## Official Sources

- Gazette of Pakistan: official government publication channel.
- Ministry of Law and Justice: `https://www.molaw.gov.pk/`
- Government of Pakistan publication channels and archived gazette references where available.

## Common Publication Types

- Act
- Ordinance
- SRO
- notification
- order
- appointment
- election gazette

## Interpretation Notes

- The gazette is the publication record, not the policy debate record.
- Do not assume every gazetted item is a law; some are administrative or procedural notices.
- A bill may have parliamentary assent before it appears in the gazette as an Act.
- Commencement can be immediate, delayed, or tied to a separate notification.

## Extraction Notes

- Preserve the official title and the date exactly as published.
- If the gazette issue includes multiple related items, keep the item boundaries clear.
- Note whether the item amends, substitutes, repeals, or merely notifies another instrument.
- For election items, capture the constituency, office, and result stage if stated.

## Common Uses

- legal status checks
- notification date checks
- election result publication checks
- service order verification
- commencement-date confirmation
